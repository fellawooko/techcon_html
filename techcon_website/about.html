History

TechCon began in 2005 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since grown into one of the most significant conferences in the industry. Over the years, it has served as a platform for groundbreaking innovations and ideas.

Mission

Our mission is to bring together tech innovators, industry leaders, and enthusiasts to share knowledge, foster collaboration, and drive innovation for a better tomorrow. TechCon is dedicated to promoting diversity and inclusivity in technology.

Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Known as the first computer programmer, Ada's groundbreaking work laid the foundation for computational science.

Alan Turing

A pioneer of artificial intelligence and computer science, Alan's contributions remain influential in the tech industry.

Grace Hopper

A trailblazer in computer programming, Grace developed the first compiler and helped shape modern computing.
